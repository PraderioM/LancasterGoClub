The Lancaster Go club is a friendly community of Go players living in Lancaster and whom first started gathering over 2 decades ago. We meet in Lancaster University (Fylde College). Our club is free and open to anyone, including players not affiliated with the university.

Do you like strategy games, intellectual challenges, complex patterns build from easy rules? Then Go is the game for you!

Go (also known as baduk, igo or weiqi) is a 2 player abstract strategy game that is believed to be the oldest continuously played game in human history.

Go is known for having very simple rules that contrast with its extreme complexity (it takes under 5 minutes to learn the rules and a lifetime to master the game).

Whether you are a complete beginner or a dan player the Lancaster Go club offers a friendly and relaxing environment to play and study Go.
